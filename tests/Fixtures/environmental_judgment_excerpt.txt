IN THE HIGH COURT OF ZIMBABWE
HARARE
HH 12-24

Between
Delta Mining (Private) Limited
and
Environmental Management Agency

The applicant seeks to set aside the suspension of its mining certificate. According to Justice Moyo, section 97 of the Environmental Management Act requires an Environmental Impact Assessment Certificate before any mining operations begin. The Environmental Management Agency advised that the applicant had commenced trenching before the certificate was issued.

─── PAGE 2 ───

The court found that the applicant failed to obtain the Environmental Impact Assessment Certificate before implementation of the project. The applicant must cease all mining activity until the certificate is issued and the rehabilitation plan is approved. Accordingly, the application is dismissed with costs.
